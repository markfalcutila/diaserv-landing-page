Why Choose Us? Diaserv Diagnostic Laboratory offers wide range of tests, modern technology and equipment, well- maintained facilities with professional and experienced staffs, timeliness, accurate, and reliable results and promotes healthy relationship with patients while maintaining data privacy that will support your healthcare journey.
Our Vision To be a top notch and state-of-the-art diagnostic facility that offers quality and cost-effective diagnostic services.
Our Mission To provide advance, precise, and reliable diagnostic services, that are available and affordable for the society.
Our Goals The clinical laboratory’s primary objective is to provide patients with affordable yet accurate and precise diagnostic service in which standards and protocols are strictly employed to assure the quality of the results which the physician will use to provide the proper diagnosis and treatment.
WHO WE ARE About Us

Diaserv has proven its marketing strength over its competitors through the use of innovative technology to automate its services. This was achieved through the acquisition of machines such as Biosystems A25 and A15 for automated chemistry, Mindray BC5120 and Beckman DXH500 for hematology, Biorad D10 and Lifotronic H8 for Glycosylated hemoglobin, Diestro for electrolyte determination, Roche Cobas u411 for automated urine strip analysis, and Roche Cobas e411, Abbott Architect i1000SR, and YHLO iFlash1200 for immunology and endocrinology tests.

Diaserv has also demonstrated inter-laboratory cooperation by serving as a provincial referral laboratory for both hospital and non-hospital based clinical laboratories. It covers a wide area, giving it a vast network of service. Areas include municipalities in Pampanga such as City of San Fernando, Angeles, Arayat, Magalang, Guagua, Lubao, Floridablanca, Macabebe, and Mabalacat, as well as neighboring provinces such as Tarlac and Bataan.

Accuracy and precision of results are highly guaranteed through daily running of controls per assay, weekly calibration of assays, and monthly maintenance of machines. The laboratory also participates in National External Quality Assurance Scheme (NEQAS) set forth by the various NRLs to ensure the reliability of results. Moreover, Diaserv is equipped with proficient, experienced, knowledgeable, skilled, sociable, and good-natured personnel.
